Bullets
An infinitely nested bullet list.
Editing
Tab / Shift+Tab indent/outdent · Enter splits at cursor · Backspace on empty deletes
Navigation
Click a bullet dot to zoom in · Use Back, Up, Home to navigate · Cmd+F to search
Features
#tags for categorization · Drag dots to reorder · Shift+Arrow to multi-select · Star bullets for quick access via menu · Paste links to embed automerge docs, YouTube, or images
