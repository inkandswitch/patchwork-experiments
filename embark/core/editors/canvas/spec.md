# Canvas

The board everything else sits on: an open space where you arrange cards,
documents, and notes, and where they coordinate through shared state.

- hosts the shared state ([core](../core)) that every card reads and writes
- comes with a parts bin of starter cards to drag out — a
  [map](automerge:4Mrgb9EZpScpUgUo2dZ2wQCJKkEn),
  [Place Finder](automerge:r1gkpehGtt4WTR1pz7mBac9SnJp),
  [Weather](automerge:2gtsy4b6hU38DQAMPk6kYHLwxrxE), and more
- answers the "where is something shaped like this?" questions cards ask, so the
  [map](automerge:4Mrgb9EZpScpUgUo2dZ2wQCJKkEn) and others can find what they need
- an embed can be a whole document or a standalone card pointed at by url
