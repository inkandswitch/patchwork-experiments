# Stickers

Draws inline annotations — "stickers" — into text editors: small notes placed
before, after, or over a span of text, or styling laid on the text itself. It
renders whatever stickers are aimed at a note; cards like the
[Unit Converter](automerge:2YXL4FwZ7crmDpgcm2FobPGpQyE7),
[Currency Converter](automerge:27NZacXx1DQVusdWaNS9US9t5spB), and
[Timer](automerge:3wGbMYtuZ7EtBvDsbuwRBcP6v7P2) are what produce them.

- a sticker can be plain text, a style on the text, or a small embedded widget
- it also ships the live countdown widget the
  [Timer](automerge:3wGbMYtuZ7EtBvDsbuwRBcP6v7P2) uses
- the same annotations can appear on
  [todo](automerge:3yXyPjHVKAJPf46W7AsVZr8NmEKP) items, not just in notes
