# Weather

A card that adds a `/weather` [command](automerge:2hQnZDS6iDYoANsjEDsRsm7HF3Po):
type `/weather berlin` in a note and it drops in today's forecast, resolved from
a [place](automerge:r1gkpehGtt4WTR1pz7mBac9SnJp) already nearby or a quick lookup.
Forecasts come from Open-Meteo.

Each forecast is a small card of its own:

- the place it's for (a link to its [place](automerge:r1gkpehGtt4WTR1pz7mBac9SnJp), not a copy)
- the day's high and low
- a short summary and emoji
