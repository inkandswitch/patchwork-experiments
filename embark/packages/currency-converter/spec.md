# Currency Converter

A card that reads your notes and annotates every foreign-currency amount with
today's value in US dollars, shown as a
[sticker](automerge:2fygfMPPm6BdmtgRXbpQvVBbizRL) right after the amount.

- handles symbols (€10, £5), codes (1000 JPY), and names (10 euro)
- rates come from the European Central Bank, via frankfurter.app
